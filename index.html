positives

Negatives

warning to the instrument dispenser The maximum output capability of the hearing instrument may exceed 132 dB SPL (IEC 711). Special care should be exercised in selecting and fitting the instrument as there may be risk of impairing the remaining hearing of the hearing instrument user

problems

Do not wear your hearing aids: In the shower, pool, or in the water, in the hairdressing salon, when applying perfume, while applying hair sprays and sunscreen, around very loud noises, you should also not wear your hearing aids when you go to bed. You will risk dropping, losing or damaging your hearing devices. Additionally, sleeping without your devices gives your ears a chance to "breathe" throughout the night, allowing air to pass through, as well as reducing the potential for more earwax to build up.

Tips for communicating with people with hearing loss

Try these tips for better communication at work Since many hard-of-hearing people can understand you better when they can see you, these communication tips can make conversations easier: Speak slowly and clearly and be prepared to repeat your words Start and end conversations while facing your colleague Do not speak with your hand in front of your mouth Do not turn your back on your colleague when speaking or presenting Avoid mumbling or whispering

Features

what is the Oticon Dynamo

Oticon Dynamo

is a BTE 13 Super Power instrument built on the Inium Sense platform, with a peak output of 139 dB SPL and a peak full-on gain of 78dB. Dynamo uses the new Inium Sense feedback shieldSP, an efficient anti-feedback system. Dynamo is designed for patients with severe-to-profound hearing loss who want a slim super power instrument, easy access to volume control, and support for individual listening preferences to ensure optimal speech understanding. Dynamo features a unique combination approach to signal processing: Speech Rescue™ performs precise frequency composition to ensure fuller access to high frequency sounds while Speech Guard E’s advanced adaptive compression preserves important speech details.

the first: feature

Speech Guard E

Speech Guard E is the amplitude compression system in Dynamo SP10 and SP8. It combines two methods of amplification, non-linear and linear, into a single compression system that helps preserve the fine details of sound - from soft to loud - making sounds audible, comfortable and clear. This compression system guards the dynamic contrast of the entire signal to let the rescued high frequency speech sounds appear more clearly at a lower frequency region.

YouMatic Premium

YouMatic is a personal automatic system programmed to the patient’s individual needs and sound preferences. YouMatic controls the sound processing across multiple environments by adjusting instrument response times, directionality, noise management, and transient management.
